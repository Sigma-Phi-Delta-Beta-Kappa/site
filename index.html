COVID-19 Statement

August 24, 2020 Julian Tsang

To our family, friends, and members of the Lehigh Community:

Like you, we have closely watched the coronavirus situation evolve over the past few months. The most difficult part is doing what is best for the Brotherhood and surrounding community, following the guidelines provided by the CDC and the state of Pennsylvania to protect the health and welfare of others.

It is in the best interest of the Leadership of the Sigma Phi Delta Engineering Fraternity to prioritize the following:

  1. To rightfully serve and protect the health of the Brotherhood and the Lehigh community.
  2. Ensure that every member upholds the values and actions that will protect against the spread of the coronavirus.

To say the least, the situation we have been given is far from easy to navigate. We've been given an opportunity to show that as a community, significant perseverance in doing what is right is the best means of ensuring the safety of all. As a fraternity, we have learned to explore new opportunities of virtual interaction that cause great function to the organization. In doing so, we hope to continue strengthening the bonds of Brotherhood and cultivate new members through a unique type of recruitment style.

-Julian Tsang, President

About Us

"The Premier International Fraternity of Engineers"

June 8, 2020 Executive Board

The Beta Kappa chapter of Sigma Phi Delta is a social and professional fraternity at Lehigh University that was founded in Spring 2013. Our goals as a fraternity are to promote the advancement of the engineering profession, encourage excellence in scholarship, and to successfully integrate rigorous academics and active social lives. Sigma Phi Delta provides a positive atmosphere to encourage personal growth and create lasting bonds of brotherhood.
